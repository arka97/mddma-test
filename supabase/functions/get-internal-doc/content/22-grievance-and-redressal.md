## 2. What we accept

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Privacy & data | Access / correction / erasure / consent withdrawal requests |
| Content takedown | Defamatory or unlawful posts, infringing listings |
| Account abuse | Impersonation, spam, harassment |
| Trade dispute (informational) | Buyer/seller misconduct — we facilitate but are **not** the arbiter |
| Refund | Forwarded under doc 21 |
| Platform bug with privacy/security impact | Suspected data leak, RLS bypass |

## 3. How to file

Send an email to **grievance@mddma.org** with:

- Your full name, registered email, phone
- A description of the grievance
- Date(s) and URLs (if applicable)
- Any supporting documents (PDF / images ≤ 10 MB total)
- For privacy rights: state which right under DPDP §11–14 you are exercising

Anonymous complaints are reviewed but may be deprioritised. Frivolous or vexatious complaints may be refused.

## 4. Timelines (legally binding)

| Stage | Maximum time | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Acknowledgement | **24 hours** | IT Rules 3(11)(b) |
| Substantive response | **15 days** | IT Rules 3(11)(c) |
| Personal-data-breach notice (if applicable) | "Without delay" — practically ≤ 72 hours | DPDP §8(6) |
| Removal of patently unlawful content (e.g. CSAM, non-consensual imagery) | **24 hours** | IT Rules 3(2)(b) |

A response may be an action, a denial with reasons, or a request for more information.

## 5. Escalation

If you are dissatisfied with our response, you may escalate to:

- **Data Protection Board of India** (under DPDP §28), once operational.
- **Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology**, Grievance Appellate Committee, for IT-Rules-related complaints (IT Rules 3A).
- **District Consumer Commission**, Mumbai, for service-deficiency claims under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019.

We will cooperate fully with any lawful enquiry from these bodies.

## 6. Internal workflow (for the office team)

1. Email arrives at `grievance@mddma.org` (monitored by Aditya Parmar; backup: Secretary).
2. Acknowledgement template sent within 24h with ticket ID `GRV-YYYYMMDD-NNN`.
3. Ticket logged in private spreadsheet (`grievance-log.xlsx`) — columns: ID, date, complainant, category, action, closed_date.
4. If action required in-app, route to admin via `/account/moderation`.
5. If action requires SQL or role change, use the recipes in doc 17.
6. Close ticket with written outcome to complainant. Retain log for 7 years.

## 7. Annual transparency note

Per IT Rules 4(1)(d), once the Platform crosses the significant-social-media-intermediary threshold (5 million users), a monthly compliance report is published. Until then, MDDMA voluntarily publishes a single annual transparency summary (counts only, no PII) covering:

- Grievances received, by category
- Grievances resolved within 15 days vs late
- Content takedown actions
- Government / law-enforcement requests received and complied with

The first such report will be published 12 months after Platform launch.
