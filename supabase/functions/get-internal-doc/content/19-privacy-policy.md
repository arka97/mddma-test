## 4. Why we process it (Lawful purposes)

Under **DPDP §6 (consent)** and **§7 (certain legitimate uses)**:

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---|---|
| Operating your account and listing | §6 consent + §7(a) voluntary provision for specified purpose |
| Connecting buyers and sellers via RFQ | §6 consent + §7(a) |
| Issuing membership invoices and processing Razorpay payments | §7(g) compliance with law (GST), §6 consent |
| KYC verification and trust badges | §6 consent; documents are processed solely to issue a tier and then minimised |
| Moderation, fraud and abuse prevention | §7(c) for the purposes of medical emergency / public order — limited use; primarily §6 consent + legitimate interest |
| Service improvement (aggregated analytics) | §7(a) voluntary use |
| Mandatory legal disclosures | §7(g) |

We do **not** sell your personal data. We do not use it for behavioural advertising on third-party platforms.

## 5. Consent

You consent when you:
- Sign up via `/login`,
- Submit `/apply` (the checkbox binds the version hash of this policy and the Terms),
- Submit an RFQ (the cart drawer states the data shared with the seller),

You can withdraw consent at any time by emailing the Grievance Officer. Withdrawal does not affect processing done before withdrawal (DPDP §6(4)).

## 8. Your rights (DPDP §11–14)

You have the right to:

1. **Access** — request a copy of your personal data.
2. **Correct & update** — edit your profile / company in-app or by request.
3. **Erase** — request deletion. Exceptions apply for legal retention (RFQs, payments).
4. **Withdraw consent** — see §5.
5. **Nominate** — appoint another person to exercise your rights in case of incapacity (DPDP §14).
6. **Grievance redressal** — contact the Grievance Officer; if unsatisfied, escalate to the **Data Protection Board of India** under DPDP §28.

To exercise any right: email **grievance@mddma.org** from the address linked to your account. We will verify your identity before acting.

## 9. Security

- All traffic is over HTTPS.
- Database access is gated by Row-Level Security policies; see doc 15.
- Passwords are hashed by the auth provider; we never see plain-text passwords.
- KYC document buckets are private with admin-only signed-URL reads.
- We do not export the database to laptops, USBs or unmanaged endpoints.

No system is perfectly secure. If we become aware of a personal data breach affecting you, we will notify you and the Data Protection Board within the timelines required by DPDP §8(6).

## 10. Cookies & similar technologies

We use only **strictly necessary** cookies (session, CSRF, auth). We do not use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. If we add analytics in future, this policy will be updated and consent will be requested.
