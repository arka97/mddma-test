# How MDDMA verifies a trader

Every member firm on the MDDMA directory carries one of four verification levels. This is what each level actually means — and what a buyer can rely on.

## The four levels

| Level | Requirement | What a buyer knows |
| ----- | ----------- | ------------------ |
| Unverified | Email only | Nothing beyond a working inbox |
| Email verified | Confirmed email domain | The email address is real |
| Company verified | GSTIN checked + company registration confirmed | The firm exists in the GST database |
| GST verified | GSTIN + PAN + physical office visit by MDDMA staff | The firm exists, files GST, and has a real office |

## The steps

1. **Apply** at [/apply](/apply) with company name, GSTIN, PAN and IEC.
2. **Automated check** — the platform pulls the GSTIN status from the GSTN portal within minutes.
3. **Documentary check** — MDDMA staff review the incorporation document (Partnership Deed, LLP Agreement, CoI) and the IEC certificate.
4. **Physical visit** (GST-verified tier only) — an MDDMA representative visits the office at APMC Vashi or the traders' declared address, photographs the signage, and files the visit report.

Turnaround is 3–5 working days for the paperwork tier and 10–15 days for the physical-visit tier.

## Why buyers care

- The verified tier appears on the member card, the storefront and every product page.
- Un-verified sellers cannot access paid features (RFQ, priority placement, branded storefront).
- Any complaint against a GST-verified member triggers a grievance case — see the [grievance policy](/documents/22-grievance-and-redressal) for the redressal timeline.

## Cost

Verification is included in the ₹10,000/year paid membership. There is no per-application fee.
